Belize (Belize in Spanish too, the second official language after English) is an American country. It was the 137th nation to join the Hattrick World.

Belize has an area of 22 970 km2 (8 867 sq mi) and 400 thousand inhabitants, with the lowest population density in Central America. Capital is Belmopan and Belize City is the largest city.
The Maya Civilization spread into the area of Belize between 1500 B.C. and A.D. 300 and flourished until about 1200. European exploration was begun in 1638, with Spain and Britain claiming to the land until it became a British colony in 1840 as British Honduras. Independence was achieved on 21 September 1981.

Global is a blanket or catch-all term used to refer to the game as a whole, as opposed to only a single country.

The global conference in an artistic concept art

Seasons

Global is a season of the game counted starting from Swedish season 1.

For instance, trying to clarify this abstruse concept, we can say that:

Canada's season 15 is actually global season 27, because this is the 27th season for the overall game, although for Canada it is only the 15th season since its integration. Again, while the whole world enters in the 33rd season of the global, in Canada they are playing still the 21st local season.

Forum

More commonly, Global is used to refer to the Global Forum (English). This place of confrontation is known for its extremely large volume of posts and threads, of which a very small percentage is intelligible and relevant. There are many cult phrases such as "English only in Global", "xxxx is evil", the evergreen "Why did I lose?", or very popular, sometimes harmful, idea topics to improve the game.

To add more grease to all this blobbish material, it should be remembered that, from time to time, also Hattrick Team post on this forum about various aspects of the game.

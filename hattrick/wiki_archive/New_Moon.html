New Moon

From Hattrick
Jump to navigationJump to search
A picture of a New Moon player before the global renaming

The New Moon was a test country out of the hattrick world.

It existed for about 16 seasons until it was casually discoved by users.

Discovery

On 05/09/2008, Gaborss found a strange player messing with id codes. He immediately wrote about on Global (English) (11815021.1).

New Moon was removed shortly after the discovery. When New Moon was deleted, all the players were renamed "John Doe".

Purpose

On New Moon Hattrick players are strange

The country had one experimental team which won the championship 6 seasons, probably managed by Hattrick Team.

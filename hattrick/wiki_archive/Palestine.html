Palestine (فلسطين‎ Filasṭīn in Arabic) is a Near East Asian country. It was the 128th nation to join the Hattrick World.

The State of Palestine is a de jure sovereign state with East Jerusalem as the designated capital, although its administrative center is currently located in Ramallah. The entirety national area has been occupied since 1948. Today Palestinian population of over 5 million claims an independent territory, while its effective jurisdiction is limited to the Gaza Strip.

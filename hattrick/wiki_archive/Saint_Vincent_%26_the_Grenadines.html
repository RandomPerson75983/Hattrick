Saint Vincent & the Grenadines (also called Saint Vincent & the Grenadines in local language) is an American country. It was the 136th nation to join the Hattrick World.

Saint Vincent & the Grenadines, is an Anglo-Caribbean Sovereign State. Kingstown is the capital and main port. Saint Vincent (also frequently known simply as Saint Vincent) has a British colonial history. Its territory consists of a main island and the northern two-thirds of the Grenadines, a chain of 32 smaller islands.
It is a densely populated country for its size of 389 km2 (150 sq mi) with approximately 110 thousand inhabitants.

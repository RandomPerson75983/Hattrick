Jordan (الأردن‎, al-ʾUrdun in Arabic) is a Near East Asian country. It was the 91st nation to join the Hattrick World.

The Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan (Arabic: المملكة الأردنية الهاشمية‎, Al-Mamlakah al-’Urdunniyyah Al-Hāshimiyyah) is a semi-arid, relatively small country with an area of 89 342 km2 (34 495 sq mi) and a population of 10 million. The capital, Amman, is Jordan's most populous city.
During World War I, the Ottoman Empire was partitioned by Britain and France. The Emirate of Transjordan was established in 1921, then it became a British protectorate. In 1946, Jordan became an independent state.

Barbados (also called Barbados in local language) is a North American country. It was the 104th nation to join the Hattrick World.

Barbados is an island country in the Lesser Antilles of the West Indies, in the Caribbean. Its capital and largest city is Bridgetown.
On 30 November 1966, Barbados became an independent state and Commonwealth realm. It has a population of 290 thousand people, predominantly of African descent.

Bosnia & Herzegovina (B&H) or Bosna i Hercegovina ( in local language, sometimes called Bosnia–Herzegovina) is a Eastern European country. It was the 60th nation to join the Hattrick World.

Bosnia–Herzegovina, often known informally as Bosnia, is composed by the inland, the Bosnia region, and a southern Mediterranean tip, the Herzegovina region. Sarajevo is the capital and largest city.
Kingdom of Bosnia was annexed into the Ottoman Empire, under whose rule it remained from the mid-15th to the late 19th centuries. This was followed by annexation into the Austro-Hungarian Monarchy, and then to Yugoslavia. Following the dissolution of Yugoslavia, the republic proclaimed independence in 1992, obtained after Bosnian War in late 1995.

History

  • B&H's league has existed since July 2003 (Global Season 20).
  • The country was restarted after an error. Due to the error, what appears as season 2 is in fact season 1, season 3 is season 2, etc. For example, the displayed season 36 was the 35th season played (note that the strange data behave is not corrected in the National trophies table below).
  • Bosnia & Herzegovina has not hosted any World Cup yet.

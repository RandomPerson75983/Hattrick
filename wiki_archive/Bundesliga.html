From Hattrick
Jump to navigationJump to search

The Bundesliga (Federal League) is the name for the premier league of any sport in Austria and Germany.

Germany

German Bundesliga at Wikipedia.
Used on its own, Bundesliga usually refers to Germany's highest football league. In Germany it was established in the the 1963/64 season.

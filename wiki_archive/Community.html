The Hattrick community is generally regarded as one of the best aspects of the game, with its large user base to interact with.

Some players hardly take part in the community at all, while others are very active.

People

Within the game, the community is built by the forum, federations, HT mail, and guest books of supporters. Flag chasing and related federations are another big part of the fun.

The many spontaneous meetings organised over the years show that the community of Hattrick doesn't end on the internet.

The numerous CHPP applications written and maintained by devoted users are another aspect of the Hattrick community.

Latehome-Radio
Latvian HT Radio

Some example of internet radio channels created by the community

There are also many other external placed devoted to the community, such as IRC Channels, the Bottom Division Federation and Football Forums and other websites.

Media

Magazines or digital fanzines are released, sometimes one issue each week, gathering news about Hattrick, articles from serious to humorous and a lot of interviews. An example is Between the Lines, but myriads of local publications have been written over the years.

There are also comic strips, i.e. Cartoon Utd. by HansJ or the corner of the hattrickaholic by teodoreh and Kostas3001.

Another incredible expression of the community passion for the game are the internet radio stations.

Game Volunteers

One very important part of the community are all the translations that have come from the hard work of the users. Also the Mods and GMs are volunteer staff and keep the game running.

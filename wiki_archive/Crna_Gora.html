Montenegro (Црна Гора, Crna Gora in Montenegrin) is an Eastern European country. It was the 111th nation to join the Hattrick World.

Montenegro is home to 600 thousand peole. Its capital and largest city is Podgorica.
During the Early Medieval period, three principalities were located on the territory then the name Montenegro started being used between 1431 and 1498. After falling under Ottoman rule, de jure independence was recognised by the Great Powers at the Congress of Berlin in 1878.
After World War I, it became part of Yugoslavia. Following the breakup of Yugoslavia, in 1991 the republics of Serbia and Montenegro together established a federation known as the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia. On the basis of an independence referendum held in May 2006, Montenegro declared independence and the federation peacefully dissolved.

History

  • Montenegrin league exists since August 2006. It is one of the four new leagues created in global season 30.
  • Montenegro was originally "Srbija i Crna Gora" (existing since April 2002), that became simply Srbija after creation of Montenegro.
  • Montenegro has hosted World Cup XIX, qualifying for the event (then NT was eliminated in Round II).

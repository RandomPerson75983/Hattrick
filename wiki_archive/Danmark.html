Denmark (Danmark in Danish) is a Northern European country. It was the 10th nation to join the Hattrick World.

The Kingdom of Denmark includes two autonomous territories in the North Atlantic Ocean: the Faroe Islands and Greenland. Land area is only 42,394 km2 (16,368 sq mi) with a population of 5.8 million, but, including islands, the total area is 2 210 579 km2 (853 509 sq mi).
Copenhagen is the nation's capital, largest city, and main commercial centre.

Qatar (دولة قطر‎, Dawlat Qaṭar in Arabic) is a Near East Asian country. It was the 121st nation to join the Hattrick World.

The State of Qatar has the highest per capita income in the world, backed by the world's third-largest natural gas reserves and oil reserves. The population is 2.6 million: 313,000 Qatari citizens and 2.3 million expatriates.
Following Ottoman rule, Qatar became a British protectorate in the early 20th century until gaining independence in 1971. Capital and largest city is Doha.

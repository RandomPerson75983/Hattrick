Helper application

From Hattrick
Jump to navigationJump to search
INTERWIKI

· English · Français · Magyar ·


A helper application is designed to help manage some team aspects, from choosing your match lineup to helping you with your training plans. Helper applications can be especially handy when you are new to Hattrick, though even the best managers can find a use for them, such as getting long term statistics about your team. All helper applications that login to Hattrick must be CHPP approved.

More pages about helper applications can be found here.

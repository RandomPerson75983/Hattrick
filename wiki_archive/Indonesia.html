Indonesia Hattrick Community

Indonesia Hattrick Community, or better known as INTRIC, is a federation for Indonesian hattrick community founded by CAPPELO. Unfortunately, not much activity is going on in INTRIC, since even the Indohattrickers (Indonesian Hattrickers) with HT-supporter prefer to talk in the Indonesian nation conference.
Intric support by many Indonesian hattrickers. At the desk, Intric organized by CAPPELO as the President, GM-Toshi as the CEO and many more poeple working together for the love of the game.


Kopdar

The IndoHattrickers have been organizing Kopdar (Kopi Daratan) which would have any IndoHattrickers to meet each other face to face. In a Kopdar, the IndoHattrickers basically discuss, talk, and share anything related, or not related to Hattrick. The usual spots for a Kopdar are either Mall Foodcourts or restaurants.


Candradimuka Cup

Candradimuka Cup, organized by dj-exile,himatika (and now handle by AnnKawaii) is the unofficial Indonesia Cup to find the new young talents for the Indonesian U20 National Team. Quite few Indonesian U20 players played in this unofficial cup. This cup however, is only applicable for those who are playing in Indonesia, and those who's team has at least three under 20 years old Indonesian players to train. This way, it's easier for the Indonesian U20 National manager to scout the new roots for the U20 National Team.
The cup itself is sponsored by GM-Toshi, who's the founder of Candradimuka Cup.


Regions

In Hattrick, Indonesia was divided into 26 regions: Ambon, Banda Aceh, Bandung, Banjarmasin, Bengkulu, Denpasar, Jakarta, Jambi, Jayapura, Kendari, Kupang, Lampung, Manado, Mataram, Medan, Padang, Palangkaraya, Palembang, Palu, Pekanbaru, Pontianak, Samarinda, Semarang, Surabaya, Ujung Pandang, Yogyakarta.

Top five regions with the most teams (incl. bot teams) are Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Medan, Yogyakarta.

Currently Indonesia is divided into 33 regions: Regions Indonesia

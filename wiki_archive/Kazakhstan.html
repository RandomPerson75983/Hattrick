Kazakhstan is a transcontinental country largely located in Middle East Asia, the 95th nation to join the Hattrick World.

Republic of Kazakhstan has an estimated 18.3 million population and economical primar resourses are oil and gas industry, and vast mineral extraction.
Since 1997 and independence from Russia, the capital is Nur-Sultan, formerly known as Astana. It was moved from Almaty, the country's largest city.

History

  • Kazakhstani league has existed since May 2005. It joined Hattrick right before the beginning of global season 45.
  • The league reached the limit of 168 users for the first time during week 9 of local season 7, all three divisions being 100% full.
The extensions to four levels was therefore made before the beginning of season 8.
At the maximum expansion, the users number reach the 50% of the 680 available teams.
  • Kazakhstan hosted U-20 World Cup XXIX. NT failed to qualify for the event.

Kyrgyzstan (Кыргызстан, Kyrgyzstan in in local language, also known as Kirghizia) is a Middle East Asian country. It was the 87th nation to join the Hattrick World.

Capital and largest city of the Kyrgyz Republic is Bishkek. Ethnic Kyrgyz (closely related to other Turkic languages) make up the majority of the country's six million people, followed by significant minorities of Uzbeks and Russians. Its
The country has periodically fallen under foreign domination as part of the Silk Road and other commercial and cultural routes and attained sovereignty as a nation-state only after the breakup of the Soviet Union in 1991. Russian remains widely spoken and is an official language

  • Kyrgyz league has been officially part of the Hattrick world since October 2004 (global season 24).
  • The name of the country was Kyrygzstan, but local community chose to renamed it in season 45 (2018).
  • Kyrgyzstan has not hosted any World Cup yet.

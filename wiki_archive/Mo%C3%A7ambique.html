Mozambique (Moçambique in Portuguese) is an African country. It was the 115th nation to join the Hattrick World.

The Republic of Mozambique has a population of around 29 million. The capital and largest city of Mozambique is Maputo.
After over four centuries of Portuguese rule, Mozambique gained independence in 1975, becoming the People's Republic of Mozambique

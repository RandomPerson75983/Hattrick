My Hattrick is the very first of seven top menu categories. Upon clicking this, a summary of what's happened recently in Hattrick will appear: manager news, latest news and staff messages, announcements and new features available to users, changes to how the game engine function, system information, which involve news items such as recent problems whilst processing games.

This page also allows existing supporters to see when their membership expires, and also includes links to the Hattrick shop in order to buy supporter membership. More information about details of the Hattrick supporter package is also available from links contained on this page.

The My Hattrick menu using the simple skin, Hattrick Supporter features included

Submenus

My Hattrick

  • News: News about Hattrick, the so-called "myHT" messages archive of Hattrick announces. .
  • Editorials: Longer announcements mostly written by the HTs.
  • Inbox: Internal mail system for users and GM messagges. Send and receive HT Mails.
  • Bookmarks : Various bookmarks.
  • Reminders : Reminders set and automatically sent by the system at the specified time.
  • Notes : Personal notes.
  • Invites: Invitation feature.
  • Mobile: Anything connected to Hattrick Mobile, a service for mobile phones.
  • Preferences: Anything that can be customised.
This is where all user account settings, security codes and passwords, preferred language, and visual appearance settings can be amended. Security codes are alternate passwords that are used to access external helper applications and are different to your main login password. These allow these helper programs to access your team data in a read-only format so that nothing bad can be done to your team that can be attributed to the external developer.

Community

  • Hattrick Press: News by the community.
  • Federations : Main page for the federation system and access to the manager's own federations.
  • Officials: The list of all current officials with a few filter options.
  • Football teams: A page with your favourite real football teams.
  • Logo Rating : A function to rate Supporter logos.
  • CHPP Programs: Everything related to CHPP.
  • Links: Links to homepages outside of Hattrick.
  • Hattrick Wiki: Hattrick's own wiki (these pages!).
  • Stage: Application form for Stage.

Old items

License

This was where you could complete the Hattrick Manager License Challenge.

Ads

The ad pages was a place to make ads for friendlies, players, youth friendlies and youth leagues.

Polls

Occasionally the Hattrick team had asked user feedback - polls targeted at you would appear here from time to time. This item was only shown when there was a poll ongoing or when there had been one recently. If there had been a new poll available, you would had got a small notice on My Hattrick.

Training reports

This was where supporters could get their training reports before reports became free for all users.

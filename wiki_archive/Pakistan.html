Pakistan (پاکستان in Urdu) is a Near East Asian country. It was the 62nd nation to join the Hattrick World.

Islamic Republic of Pakistan is the world's fifth-most populous country with a population exceeding 212.2 million. By area, it is the 33rd-largest country, spanning 881,913 square kilometres (340,509 square miles).
Pakistan was a dominion created for Indian Muslims in 1947 through the partition of India. In 1956 it became an Islamic republic. The territory has been ruled by numerous empires, including ancient and modernn Persians, ancient Macedonians, Indians, Arabs, Mongols, the Mughal Empire, Afghans and, most recently, the British Indian Empire.

History

  • Pakistani league has existed since July 2003. It was one of the six new countries created in global season 20.
  • The country was restarted after an error. Due to the error, what appears as season 2 is in fact season 1, season 3 is season 2, etc. For example, the displayed season 36 was the 35th season played (note that the strange data behave is not corrected in the National trophies table below).
  • Pakistan hosted U-20 World Cup XXV, but NT failed to qualify for the event.

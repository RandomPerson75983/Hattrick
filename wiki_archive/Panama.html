Panama (Panamá in Spanish, obsolete) is a North American country. It was the 81st nation to join the Hattrick World.

The Republic of Panama is home to nearly half the country's 4 million people. The capital and largest city is Panama City. Revenue from tolls of the Panama Canal, construction completed by the USA in 1914 and transfer to Panama in 2000, represents a significant portion of Panama's GDP.
Spanish colonists arrived in the 16th century. It broke away from Spain in 1821 and, with the backing of the USA, Panama became independent in 1903.

History

  • Panamanian league has existed since Feb. 2004. It is one of the new countries, along with other six, created before global season 23.
  • It had initially 10 regions, but the regional partition was then extended to 13.
  • In 2009, the top division was renamed from ANAPROF (Asociación Nacional Pro Fútbol) to LPF (Liga Panameña de Fútbol), reflecting the change in real world .
